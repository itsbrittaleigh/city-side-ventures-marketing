City Side Ventures is a different investment firm that is managed by a unique, distinct and highly diverse group of seasoned and accomplished entrepreneurs. We are an ideal partner for early-stage tech-based startup companies and have a deep appreciation and understanding of what it takes to build businesses.

We want to be involved on the ground floor, at the beginning with the startup companies. We assert that by putting our experience, expertise, our sweat, and capital to work, we can succeed in building great companies.

Our business mindset is that it takes a village to launch a startup. We are not looking to invest in cars, we are looking to invest in drivers. Confident, aggressive, open minded drivers, with an endless energy and passion for success.

If you are this kind of a driver, get in touch with us today.
